Vignoble de l'Orpailleur

The Vignoble de l'Orpailleur is the great adventure of four enthusiasts who planted their roots in 1982 in the magnificent terroir of Dunham, the cradle of viticulture in Quebec. Through passion, the vines have grown, the wines have developed, and these four instigators of the Brome-Missisquoi Wine Route have developed one of the best known and most appreciated wines of Quebec.

Ste-Pétronille - Winery & Cidery

Ste-Pétronille Winery & Cidery is a hidden gem located in the charming village of Sainte-Pétronille, Quebec, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes and the scenic views of the St. Lawrence River. This winery is not just a place to taste exceptional wines and ciders; it is an experience that immerses you in the rich culture of local viticulture and gastronomy. The winery is celebrated for its meticulously crafted wines that reflect the terroir of the region.

Visit Official Website

Domaine Cotes d'Ardoise - Winery & Cidery

The Domaine des Côtes d'Ardoise, located in Dunham, is a true countryside gem. You'll be captivated by its beautiful gardens, historic buildings, and stunning outdoor art exhibit. With great pride, we invite you to enjoy tastings of our wines and ciders on-site, many of which have earned recognition over the years.
